How dried-out is the rock?

The dryness curve is the same decayed rain-load model that scores the crags, sampled every 6 hours — it reflects the last ~5 days of seepage-relevant moisture, so an old storm that has fully dried won't drag it down while recent rain will. Bars are daily rainfall. Per-crag % is how dried-out each wall is right now, given its own drainage and steepness.
